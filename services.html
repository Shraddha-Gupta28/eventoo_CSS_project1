FAQs

How do I get a quote?

You can get a quote easily by clicking on any “Get Quote” or “Contact” button available on our website. Fill out the basic form with your details, and our team will reach out to you within 24 hours to discuss your requirements in detail.

Can I customize a package?

Yes, absolutely! 🎉 We create customized packages tailored to your preferences. Just share your event date, guest count, location, and budget range — our experts will design the best package that suits your needs perfectly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including UPI, Credit/Debit cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery. Flexible payment options are available to make your booking process smooth and hassle-free.

Do you provide support after booking?

Yes! 🙌 Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you even after your booking is completed. Whether you want to make changes, upgrade your package, or need on-ground assistance, we’ve got you covered.
